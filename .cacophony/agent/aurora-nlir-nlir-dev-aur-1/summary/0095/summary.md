## What landed
- examples/golf-aur1-82-definetopic.sh — DEFINE THE TOPIC: `>#x` — # pulls the SUBJECT out of a
  sentence (the concept it's really about), > expands THAT into a full explanation. So a message
  that merely USES a concept (often while complaining) becomes a clear write-up OF the concept. "our
  closures keep capturing the loop variable…" → #x "Closure capturing the loop variable" → >#x a
  full explanation of the JS loop-capture pitfall (and its fixes). The ORDER is the trick: # runs
  FIRST (collapse to topic, drop the complaint), > runs SECOND (define it). Distinct from #55
  deep-dive (>~x expands the CONTENT of what was said) — this expands the SUBJECT of it: "X is
  broken again" → "here's what X actually is."
